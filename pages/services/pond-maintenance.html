Spring Pond Opening

After winter, your pond needs attention to get back in shape. Our spring opening service includes:

  • Removing winter debris and dead vegetation
  • Checking and restarting pumps and filters
  • Water quality testing and treatment
  • Plant care and trimming
  • Fish health check

Fall Pond Closing

Properly preparing your pond for winter prevents damage and makes spring easier. Our fall closing includes:

  • Removing leaves and debris
  • Netting or covering the pond
  • Winterizing equipment
  • Preparing fish for cold weather
  • Final water quality check

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule maintenance?

Most ponds benefit from monthly maintenance during peak season (spring through fall) and less frequent checkups in winter. We'll recommend a schedule based on your pond size, location, and specific needs.

What's the difference between maintenance and cleaning?

Maintenance is ongoing care to prevent problems. Cleaning is a deeper service when buildup has already occurred. Many customers combine both: regular maintenance plus an annual deep cleaning.
