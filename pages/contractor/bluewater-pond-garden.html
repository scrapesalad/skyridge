Overview

Bluewater Pond & Garden specializes in residential and light commercial ponds, koi ponds, and naturalistic water features. With over 10 years of experience, we handle everything from annual cleanouts to full pond redesigns.

Our team is passionate about creating and maintaining beautiful, healthy water features that enhance your outdoor living space. We understand that every pond is unique, and we tailor our services to meet your specific needs and goals.
