## üìù Customization Tips by Industry

### Service-Based (Most Common)

**Template Works Best For:**
- Contractors (all types)
- Home services
- Professional services
- Health & wellness
- Automotive services

**Key Sections:**
- ‚úÖ Service pages
- ‚úÖ City/location pages
- ‚úÖ How It Works
- ‚úÖ Lead capture forms
- ‚úÖ Before/After galleries

---

### Retail/Product-Based

**Requires More Modification:**
- Add product catalogs
- Shopping cart (if e-commerce)
- Inventory display
- Pricing tables

**Better Suited For:**
- Service providers
- Local businesses
- Lead generation

---

### Hybrid Models

**Examples:**
- Auto shop (service + parts)
- Spa (services + products)
- Salon (services + retail)

**Approach:**
- Focus on services as primary
- Add products as secondary
- Use service pages for main offerings
- Add shop page for products

## üöÄ Success Formula

**Regardless of Niche, Follow This Formula:**

1. **Clear Value Proposition**
   - What you do
   - Who you serve
   - Why choose you

2. **Strong CTAs**
   - Free quote/consultation
   - Emergency service
   - Book now
   - Call today

3. **Social Proof**
   - Testimonials
   - Reviews
   - Before/After
   - Certifications

4. **Local Optimization**
   - City pages
   - Local keywords
   - Service areas
   - Google Business

5. **Easy Contact**
   - Phone prominent
   - Contact forms
   - Chat (optional)
   - Multiple touch points
